THE 10 STEP KOREAN SKINCARE ROUTINE



1

OIL-BASED CLEANSER
The first step to your skincare routine is an oil-based cleanser. Oil cleansers are beneficial for all skin types, whether you have dry, combination, or oily skin. They remove makeup, dissolve sebum, and melt excess oils and bacteria.



2

WATER-BASED CLEANSER
Double cleansing is the way to go! Water-based cleansers can either be a foam, for more oily skin, or a gel, for more dry and sensitive skin. Using a water-based cleanser after an oil-based cleanser will remove any remaining impurities.



3

EXFOLIATOR
Exfoliating removes dead skin cells from the surface of your face. This makes it easier for your skin to absorb the rest of your skincare products, making them more effective. Keep in mind that you don't necessarily have to exfoliate everyday.



4

TONER
Toner is a watery substance that provides instant hydration and cleansing. Using a toner balances the skin’s pH, removes impurities, and helps the skin absorb other skincare products. Apply toner with a cotton pad or your hands.



5

ESSENCE
Essence is a toner/serum hybrid used to hydrate and repair the skin before applying serum. It treats wrinkles, fine lines, dullness, and uneven skin tone. Apply essence to freshly toned skin and pat it in.



6

SERUM
Serums and ampoules are essentially concentrated essences. They are packed with nutrients that directly treat specific skin concerns. Whether it's dull skin, large pores, pigmentation, wrinkles, or acne, serums are the ideal skin-perfecting step.



7

SHEET MASK
Sheet masks are a convenient way to moisturize the skin. Put one on and relax for 15 to 20 minutes while the mask moisturizes, nourishes, or brightens your skin. Sheet masks can be used everyday, but once or twice a week is sufficient.



8

EYE CREAM
Eye cream is used to hydrate, repair, and protect the area around the eyes. The skin around the eyes is very delicate, so to prevent the signs of aging, an eye cream is necessary. They help with wrinkles, dryness, and dark circles.



9

MOISTURIZER
Hydration is key to dewy, glowing skin. Moisturizers come in many forms (emulsions, lotions, gels, creams), all of which seal in moisture to plump up the skin and smooth away fine lines. Moisturizers should be used everyday, both in the morning and at night!



10

SUN PROTECTION
Sun protection is the most effective way to prevent the skin from aging. UV rays damage the skin, leading to uneven skin tone, dryness, and wrinkles. Apply sun protection every morning, and don't forget to reapply throughout the day!
